Volunteer FAQ

Before you apply.

Can anyone work directly with children?

No. Roles involving children should include appropriate screening, orientation, supervision and safeguarding expectations.

Can I help with professional skills?

Yes. Design, communications, logistics, administration, education and other responsible skills can support the work behind the scenes.

Can a company organize a group activity?

Potentially. Activities should be planned with the organization so they meet real needs and protect children’s safety, privacy and dignity.
