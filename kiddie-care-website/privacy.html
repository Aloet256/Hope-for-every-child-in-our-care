Privacy notice

Respecting personal information.

A clear starting point for how this website should handle enquiries, volunteer applications and donor details.

This website is provided as a static front-end. Form submissions are currently stored only in the user’s browser and are not transmitted to Kiddie Care until an official form service or database is connected.

Information collected

Forms may ask for names, email addresses, phone numbers, areas of interest and messages. Only information needed to respond to the enquiry should be collected.

How information should be used

Information should be used to respond to enquiries, coordinate volunteers or partnerships, provide verified donation instructions and manage safeguarding concerns. It should not be sold.

Children’s information

Do not submit unnecessary identifying or sensitive information about a child through a general website form. Safeguarding concerns should be handled through a secure, designated reporting process once established.

Storage and security

Before publication, forms should be connected to a secure service with access controls, appropriate retention periods and a process for deletion requests.

Photography

Images should be used with appropriate consent and in a way that protects dignity, privacy and safety. Requests concerning an image should be directed to the organization.

Contact

Use the contact page for privacy questions or requests relating to personal information.
