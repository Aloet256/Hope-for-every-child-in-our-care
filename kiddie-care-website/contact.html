Common questions

Helpful information before you reach out.

Can I visit an activity?

Visits involving children should be arranged in advance and follow safeguarding, privacy and supervision requirements.

Can I donate items?

Yes, when coordinated with the team so items are useful, safe, age-appropriate and aligned with current needs.

Can I use your photos?

Please request permission. Images of children should not be copied, republished or used outside their intended context.
